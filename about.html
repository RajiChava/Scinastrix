Introduction

Scinatrix is an IT solutions company that was founded in 2014. We work with large corporations to provide IT solutions in sales, finance, marketing, and operations. Our main focus is on providing advisory services, implementation, training, and support to our clients. We strive to help businesses improve their performance and adapt to the changing market needs, making them more agile and competitive. We hire and retain the best talent in the industry to generate next-gen solutions that give our clients the best competitive edge.

Innovative and Enthusiastic

At Sinastrix, we are passionate about developing innovative and advanced technology solutions that enable businesses to thrive. Our goal is to provide our clients with an exceptional experience, starting from the initial consultation and extending to the finished product.

Our team consists of skilled and experienced professionals who are committed to delivering the best quality service and ensuring customer satisfaction. We continuously stay up-to-date with the latest technological advancements to provide our clients with the most advanced solutions available. We are enthusiastic about supporting our clients in achieving their objectives and equipping them with the necessary tools to succeed.

Grow With Us

We are a global, SaaS, B2B IT Staffing start-up with offices in America and India, and we're passionate about transforming the way companies do business together. We believe that the people are the key to creating a workplace that's more than just work.

Our team is diverse, and we embrace and celebrate individuality and creativity. We foster a positive, friendly, and teamwork-oriented environment where everyone's contribution is valued.

As we continue to grow in the ever-evolving market, Scinastrix offers exciting opportunities for those seeking a dynamic and collaborative work environment. Join us and be part of our mission to drive positive change in the industry.
